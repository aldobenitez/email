World-Server Update

New network login requirements

With the recent world server deployment, a different login password is now required. Make sure you are using your ivao website password as opposed to your ‘IVAN Password’ for connecting to World Servers for both Controllers and Pilots. The new worlserver infrastructure on IVAO enables the network to manage thousands of users without causing issues of dropping connections as previously experience with the old SHARD servers.

Forgot passwords?

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Enter VID and click 'forgot password'
  3. An email will be sent with your login credentials

Recover VID or account

Contact xa-mc@ivao.aero - provide your full name and (if able) the email address so we can allocate your account.

Account is flagged inactive – can’t login?

  1. Go to: http://ivao.aero/members/person/details.asp?Id=xxxxxx (replace xxxxxx with your VID)
  2. Click ‘Reactive Account’
  3. Contact members@ivao.aero if you still experience issues.
